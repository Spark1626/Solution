About Us

We are a leading provider of IT solutions and staff augmentation solutions based out of St. Petersburg, FL. At QualRec Solutions, we understand that change is not always easy. We’ve been helping companies of all sizes respond to industry transitions in order to stay competitive. Our years of experience have taught us to always make your business success our priority.

We are in business to help you maintain your competitive advantage by cost-effectively delivering highly skilled consultants when you need them most. Our Solutions division provides life cycle technology services to help companies get past their hold-ups. Our solutions prepare businesses for tomorrow while ensuring the business is part of a current, compatible, and productive IT infrastructure. Understanding your goals is the first step towards achieving them. Understanding your business, your culture, and your needs is our business.

QualRec helps you address your IT solutions and resource requirements with a contract, contract-to-hire, and direct hire recruiting services. We invite you to see the difference by working with us. Our strength is in our team, and we are ready to work for you. We’ll keep your business on track. We’ll find the right professionals with the right skills so you can get on with the business.

Our team of experts is ready to help you develop strategies for not only surviving but thriving in the future. Give us a call today to set up your first consultation.

Anyone who’s been looking for a job or searching for a new team member to join their company knows that the job market can be unbelievably overwhelming. That is where we come in. Having mastered the art of IT solutions and staffing, we help our clients and candidates achieve their goals. Get in touch with us to learn what we can do for you.
